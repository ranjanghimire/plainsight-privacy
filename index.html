Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

PlainSight is designed to be a calm, private space for your thoughts. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync it. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

PlainSight does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers
  • Notes or content you create

All notes and data you create inside PlainSight remain stored locally on your device by default. We do not have access to them.

Optional Sync (Paid Feature)

If you choose to enable the optional paid sync feature, your notes will be securely stored and synced through your chosen provider. Sync is off by default, and enabling it is entirely your choice.

We do not store or access your synced data. Your sync provider’s privacy policy applies to any data stored with them.

In‑App Purchases

PlainSight uses RevenueCat to process in‑app purchases. RevenueCat does not collect personal information about you or your notes. It only stores the minimum data required to verify your purchase status.

No Third‑Party Tracking

PlainSight contains:

  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No tracking
  • No third‑party data sharing

Children’s Privacy

PlainSight does not collect any data and is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
